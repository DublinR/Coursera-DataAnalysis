\subsection{Setting a seed}
Setting a seed ensures reproducible results from random processes in R
\begin{verbatim}
set.seed(12345)
rnorm(5,mean=0,sd=1)
[1] 0.5855 0.7095 -0.1093 -0.4535 0.6059
set.seed(12345)
rnorm(5,mean=0,sd=1)
[1] 0.5855 0.7095 -0.1093 -0.4535 0.6059
\end{verbatim}
